# Google Maps Scraper Tips

## Start Small First

For testing, do not begin with maximum coverage.

Good QA setup:

- one keyword
- one location
- `1-3` pages

## Use One Clean Keyword Per Task

Best results usually come from one clear business concept per run.

Examples:

- `dentist`
- `coffee shop`
- `salon`

Avoid stacking many close synonyms into one task.

## Use City-Level Locations

For most business discovery use cases, city or regional locations perform better than extremely tiny areas.

Good examples:

- `New York`
- `Liverpool`
- `Tokyo`

## Use This Template as the Discovery Step

This template is strongest when used first in a workflow.

Common next steps:

- use `Page_URL` for Google Maps review workflows
- use `Website` for contact enrichment workflows


## Multi-City Workflow Tip

If you need several cities, treat each city as its own task.

Example:

- Task 1: restaurants in New York
- Task 2: restaurants in Boston
- Task 3: restaurants in Chicago

This keeps data cleaner and easier to compare.
