# Google Maps Scraper

## Keywords

- google maps scraping
- google maps business data
- google maps leads
- local business scraping

## Description

Extract business listings and contact details from Google Maps.

## Target Site

- Name: Google Maps
- URL: [https://www.google.com/maps](https://www.google.com/maps)

## Page Type

- list + detail

## Supported Languages

- en
- ja
- es
- fr
- de
- it
- ko

## Performance

Average processing speed:

- 170-230 results per minute

Note:

Runtime may vary depending on:

- Google Maps response speed
- CAPTCHA interruptions
- whether `Get Q&As` is enabled
- The size of the location input

- lead generation
